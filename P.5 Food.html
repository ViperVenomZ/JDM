The unique foods of Tokyo

Ramen!

The very popular ramen! In Tokyo, ramen is at it's best, you never tasted the best ramen until now. You can find it just about everywhere in the city, and you can customise it to your likings, or choose the most popular flavours, ranging in all kinds of broths, veggies, meats, and seasonings.

Wagyu Beef

The best beef in the world! Excited? However, if you can afford the expensive price it comes in, and in Tokyo City it's the best place eat Wagyu beef. They're usually served in high-class luxurious resturants, for those with deep pockets. The beautiful Wagyu beef will literally melt in your mouth, yeah, it's that tender!

Sushi

With one best and largest fish market located in Tokyo, you will find the best Sushi you've never tasted before. Since this is Tokyo, you will have every types of sushi available to you! And you should never be concerned about not having sushi nearby, sushi is everywhere! Why not jump in the Sake train too?
