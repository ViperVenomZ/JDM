Fun things to do in Tokyo City

The real life Super Mario Kart!

You heard it! You can actually explore Tokyo city in a Kart, and you get along with other Mario karters. It doesn't have to specifically be Mario Kart themed, you can come in dressed up and kart kitted in just about anything!
