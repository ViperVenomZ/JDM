What makes Tokyo one of the best?

The largest city

Tokyo takes the crown for being the largest city to exist. With that said, you already know there's everything to expect. Hosting over 36 million people with endless span of buildings, all ranging in different sizes and shapes.

One of the safest cities to live in

Praised many times by tourists and the residents as the one of the safest cities to live in. This comes with the little to none crime rate, this continues with extreme cleanliness, and the regulated laws play a good role here.

There's literally everything!

There is so much you can do in Tokyo city. The cuture is vibrant, and is always expanding! You can find some of the craziest conventions, and there's something for everything. The city is colourful, do what your heart desires!
