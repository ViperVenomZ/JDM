Attractions

Tokyo Tower

The Tokyo Tower is in very popular amongst the tourist. The tower looks very similar to the architecture design of the Eiffel Tower in France, Paris. The tower serves the purposes mainly in communications, supporting antennas for broadcasting, radio, and such. It was built in the year 1958.

Senso ji

A temple in Tokyo city, the oldest with a long history. Also considered to be one the most important temples in Japan. It's incredibly popular to tourist, as stats value 30 million visitors every year. The temple represents the rebirth and peace of Japanese people. It also contains other shrines with their own symbols.

Tokyo Skytree

Tokyo Skytree gives people the opportunity reach heights over Tokyo. The tower is used for broadcasting and observation, it also houses resturants. During the nights it illuminates in different colours. The building is created to resist earthquakes. Tokyo Skytrees stands at 2,080 ft
