Tokyo's Public Transports

The Bullet Train

The Japanese bullet train also known as the Shinkansen is invented for speed, traveling at 200+MPH all over Japan. The train is never late, and provides comfort, service and safety. The japanese railway system is almost perfect. Travel through Tokyo city at over 200MPH!

Tokyo Taxis

The taxis can take you any where you want in Tokyo city, and they all come in all types of shapes and sizes, coming in incredible service rates, you will always have a taxi close by. Even a little 6 year can jump into a taxi without any concerns!

Tokyo Subway System

It would crazy for you to own car if you're a permenant resident of Tokyo, as the Japanese public transport can save you money! Like the Shinkansen, the subway system is reliable with time and can get you any where within Tokyo, the train avrrivals are always frequent, so you will never miss it!
